## What it is
Actions are for long-running tasks that need feedback and can be cancelled. They combine topics and services: you send a goal, receive periodic feedback, and eventually get a result.

## When to use
- Navigation (go to point X, get progress updates)
- Manipulation (pick up object, track progress)
- Any task that takes time and needs monitoring

## Key sections
- Action servers and clients
- Goal, Feedback, Result
- Action definitions (.action files)
- Cancelling goals
