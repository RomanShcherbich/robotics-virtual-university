# MoveIt 2

## Link
https://moveit.picknik.ai/main/index.html

## What it is
MoveIt 2 is the motion planning framework for robotic arms in ROS2. It handles path planning, kinematics, collision checking, and manipulation.

## When to use
- Robot arm motion planning
- Pick and place operations
- Collision avoidance for manipulators
- Inverse kinematics
